About the Artist

YouTube Channel Instagram

Sagar Narvekar is an exceptionally talented voice artist who has captivated audiences for over a decade with his incredible ability to mimic beloved cartoon characters. With more than 10 years of professional experience, Sagar brings life to characters like Doraemon, Shin Chan, Nobita, Motu Patlu, and Chhota Bheem with unmatched precision and humor.

His passion for dubbing and performing started in childhood and grew into a full-fledged career, making him one of the most versatile and entertaining voice artists in the industry. Sagar's performances are known for their energy, authenticity, and ability to connect with audiences of all ages.
